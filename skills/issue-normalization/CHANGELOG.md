## [0.1.0] - 2026-03-29

### Added
- Initial `issue-normalization` skill focused on converting raw signals into normalized issue drafts before shaping or execution.
- A hybrid intake workflow with explicit boundaries against brainstorming, clarify, prioritization, decomposition, review-only work, and implementation planning.
- Methodology and output-template references covering provenance, issue boundaries, canonical fields, uncertainty handling, and split recommendations.
- Lightweight trigger and qualitative eval fixtures plus projection settings for cross-platform installs.
