## [0.1.0] - 2026-03-29

### Added
- Initial `issue-shaping` skill focused on turning normalized issues into bounded, execution-ready work items.
- A manual-first shaping workflow with explicit boundaries against issue intake, prioritization, decomposition, clarify, and implementation planning.
- Methodology and output-template references covering shaping questions, readiness states, non-goals, acceptance criteria, execution-brief seeds, and decomposition recommendations.
- Lightweight trigger and qualitative eval fixtures plus projection settings for cross-platform installs.
