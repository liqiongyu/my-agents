## Quality Gates

Before delivering the report, verify:

- the sample met the promised depth target or was explicitly narrowed
- actual skill content was read, not just metadata
- comparison is systematic rather than ad hoc
- standout ideas are attributed to specific sources
- the fusion strategy is actionable for the next workflow
- source URLs are included for traceability

Keep the Fusion Report separate from the target skill package unless the user
explicitly asked to collapse research and creation after reviewing the candidate
set.
